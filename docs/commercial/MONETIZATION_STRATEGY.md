## Overview

This document outlines BenchSight's monetization strategy, including pricing models, revenue projections, customer acquisition strategy, and go-to-market plan.

**Target Market:** Youth, junior, and college hockey teams  
**Positioning:** Professional-grade analytics at youth prices

---

## Pricing Models

### Subscription Tiers

#### Free Tier

**Price:** $0/month  
**Target:** Individual users, small teams, trial users

**Features:**
- 1 team
- Basic player statistics
- Limited game tracking (5 games/month)
- Basic dashboard access
- Community support

**Limitations:**
- No advanced metrics (xG, WAR/GAR)
- No export functionality
- No API access
- Limited data retention (30 days)

**Purpose:**
- User acquisition
- Product trial
- Market education

#### Team Tier

**Price:** $50/month ($500/year if paid annually)  
**Target:** Single teams, small programs

**Features:**
- 1 team
- Unlimited games
- All player statistics
- Advanced metrics (xG, WAR/GAR)
- Full dashboard access
- Export functionality (CSV, Excel)
- Email support
- 1 year data retention

**Value Proposition:**
- Professional analytics at affordable price
- Complete team analytics
- Easy to use

#### Pro Tier

**Price:** $150/month ($1,500/year if paid annually)  
**Target:** Larger programs, multiple teams, coaches with multiple teams

**Features:**
- Up to 5 teams
- Unlimited games
- All Team tier features
- Advanced analytics (RAPM, predictive)
- Custom reports
- API access
- Priority support
- Unlimited data retention
- Team management tools
- User invitations (up to 10 users)

**Value Proposition:**
- Multi-team management
- Advanced features
- Priority support
- API access for integrations

#### Enterprise Tier

**Price:** Custom pricing (starting at $500/month)  
**Target:** Leagues, large organizations, multiple programs

**Features:**
- Unlimited teams
- All Pro tier features
- League-wide analytics
- White-label options
- Dedicated support
- Custom integrations
- Advanced permissions
- SLA guarantees
- On-premise option (if needed)

**Value Proposition:**
- League-wide solution
- Custom features
- Dedicated support
- Enterprise-grade reliability

### Pricing Comparison

| Tier | Price/Month | Price/Year | Teams | Target Market |
|------|------------|-----------|-------|---------------|
| **Free** | $0 | $0 | 1 | Trial users |
| **Team** | $50 | $500 | 1 | Single teams |
| **Pro** | $150 | $1,500 | 5 | Multi-team programs |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Custom | Unlimited | Leagues |

**Annual Discount:** 17% discount for annual payments (2 months free)

### Usage-Based Pricing (Alternative)

**Consideration:** Per-game pricing for teams that track few games

**Model:**
- Base: $25/month
- Per game: $2/game
- Cap: $150/month maximum

**Pros:**
- Lower barrier to entry
- Pay for what you use

**Cons:**
- More complex billing
- Lower revenue per customer
- Harder to predict revenue

**Decision:** Start with subscription model, consider usage-based if needed

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**Assumptions:**
- Marketing spend: $2,000/month (Year 1)
- Sales time: 2 hours per customer (average)
- Sales cost: $50/hour = $100 per customer
- Marketing cost per customer: $40 (Year 1), $20 (Year 2+)

**CAC Calculation:**
- **Year 1:** $100 (sales) + $40 (marketing) = **$140 per customer**
- **Year 2+:** $100 (sales) + $20 (marketing) = **$120 per customer**

### Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Assumptions:**
- Average subscription: $75/month (mix of tiers)
- Average customer lifetime: 24 months (based on 4% monthly churn)
- Gross margin: 80% (SaaS margins)

**LTV Calculation:**
- **Monthly revenue:** $75
- **Annual revenue:** $900
- **Lifetime revenue:** $1,800 (24 months)
- **LTV (gross margin):** $1,440

**LTV:CAC Ratio:**
- **Year 1:** $1,440 / $140 = **10.3:1** (excellent)
- **Year 2+:** $1,440 / $120 = **12:1** (excellent)

**Target:** LTV:CAC > 3:1 (we exceed this significantly)

### Break-Even Analysis

**Monthly Costs (Year 1):**
- Infrastructure: $700/month
- Marketing: $2,000/month
- Development: $0 (founder time)
- Support: $500/month (part-time)
- **Total:** $3,200/month

**Break-Even Point:**
- **Conservative:** Month 8 (64 teams)
- **Realistic:** Month 6 (43 teams)
- **Optimistic:** Month 3 (29 teams)

**Year 1 Profitability:**
- **Conservative:** Break-even by end of year
- **Realistic:** Profitable by month 8
- **Optimistic:** Profitable by month 6

**Cost:** 1 month free per referral  
**ROI:** High (low cost, high conversion)

### Sales Process

#### Self-Service (Free & Team Tiers)

**Process:**
1. User discovers BenchSight (marketing)
2. Signs up for free tier
3. Tries product
4. Upgrades to Team tier (if satisfied)
5. Annual upgrade option

**Conversion Funnel:**
- Visitor → Signup: 5%
- Signup → Active User: 40%
- Active User → Paid: 10%
- **Overall:** 0.2% conversion rate

#### Assisted Sales (Pro & Enterprise)

**Process:**
1. Lead generation (marketing)
2. Initial contact (email/phone)
3. Demo/consultation
4. Proposal
5. Negotiation
6. Close

**Conversion Funnel:**
- Lead → Demo: 30%
- Demo → Proposal: 50%
- Proposal → Close: 40%
- **Overall:** 6% conversion rate

**Sales Cycle:**
- Pro tier: 1-2 weeks
- Enterprise: 1-3 months

## Revenue Optimization Strategies

### 1. Annual Payments

**Strategy:**
- Offer 17% discount for annual payments
- Encourage annual subscriptions
- Improve cash flow

**Impact:**
- Reduces churn (annual commitment)
- Improves cash flow
- Reduces payment processing costs

### 2. Upselling

**Strategy:**
- Free → Team: Highlight advanced features
- Team → Pro: Multi-team management
- Pro → Enterprise: League-wide features

**Tactics:**
- In-app prompts
- Email campaigns
- Feature limitations on lower tiers

### 3. Add-Ons

**Potential Add-Ons:**
- Additional teams: $25/month per team
- Video storage: $10/month per 100GB
- API access: $50/month (if not in Pro)
- Custom reports: $100 one-time
- Training/consulting: $500/session

**Impact:**
- Increases ARPU
- Provides flexibility
- Meets specific customer needs

### 4. Volume Discounts

**Strategy:**
- Leagues: 20% discount for 10+ teams
- Organizations: 30% discount for 25+ teams
- Custom pricing for 50+ teams

**Impact:**
- Attracts larger customers
- Increases total revenue
- Reduces per-customer support cost
