üéØ Key Methodologies

Corsi & Fenwick

Originally developed by blogger Vic Ferrari, named after Buffalo Sabres goalie Jim Corsi. Measures shot attempt differential as a proxy for puck possession.

Expected Goals (xG)

Probability-based model assigning goal likelihood to each shot based on location, shot type, game state, and other factors. First widely used in soccer analytics.

Zone Entry/Exit Tracking

Pioneered by Corey Sznajder's "All Three Zones" project. Manual tracking of how teams gain and maintain offensive zone possession.

Cycle Play Detection

BenchSight's original algorithm for identifying sustained offensive zone possession through consecutive passes. Inspired by video coaching analysis.
