CF Corsi For

All shot attempts (shots + misses + blocks) by a team while a player is on ice.

Shots + Missed Shots + Blocked Shots

CA Corsi Against

All shot attempts by the opposing team while a player is on ice.

CF% Corsi Percentage

Share of total shot attempts belonging to a player's team. Above 50% means controlling play.

CF / (CF + CA) √ó 100

FF Fenwick For

Unblocked shot attempts (shots + misses). Excludes blocked shots as they may indicate defensive positioning.

Shots + Missed Shots

FF% Fenwick Percentage

Share of unblocked shot attempts.

FF / (FF + FA) √ó 100

xG Expected Goals

Probability-weighted sum of scoring chances based on shot quality (location, type, situation).

SCF Scoring Chances For

Shot attempts from high-probability scoring areas (slot, inner slot).

Shot attempts from the most dangerous areas directly in front of the net.

Sum of shooting % and save %. Expected to regress to 100 over time.
