# Go-to-Market Strategy

Plan BenchSight's commercial launch and growth.

## Target Markets

### Phase 1: Recreational Hockey
- **NORAD League** (current)
- Beer leagues
- Adult recreational
- **TAM:** 500K+ players in NA

### Phase 2: Youth Hockey
- Travel teams
- High school hockey
- AAA/AA programs
- **TAM:** 500K+ players

### Phase 3: Junior/College
- USHL, NAHL, BCHL
- NCAA D1/D3
- Canadian junior
- **TAM:** 10K+ teams

### Phase 4: Professional
- Minor pro (AHL, ECHL)
- European leagues
- NHL (long-term)
- **TAM:** 500+ teams

## Launch Strategy

### Soft Launch (Current)
- NORAD league beta
- Gather feedback
- Iterate rapidly
- Build case studies

### Public Beta
- Open registration
- Free tier launch
- Social media presence
- Content marketing

### Commercial Launch
- Pro tier available
- Marketing campaign
- Press outreach
- Partnership announcements

## Marketing Channels

### Content Marketing
- Blog: Analytics insights
- YouTube: Tutorial videos
- Twitter/X: Stats and highlights
- Reddit: r/hockey engagement

### Partnerships
- League partnerships
- Media integrations
- Fantasy sports
- Equipment brands

### Community
- Discord server
- User forums
- Beta tester program
- Ambassador program

## Growth Metrics

### Key KPIs
- Monthly Active Users (MAU)
- Paid conversion rate
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- Churn rate

### Growth Targets
| Phase | Timeline | MAU Target | MRR Target |
|-------|----------|------------|------------|
| Beta | Month 1-3 | 100 | $0 |
| Launch | Month 4-6 | 1,000 | $1K |
| Growth | Month 7-12 | 10,000 | $10K |
| Scale | Year 2 | 100,000 | $100K |

## Competitive Positioning

### Differentiators
1. **Real tracking data** (not just box scores)
2. **Recreational focus** (underserved market)
3. **Modern UX** (better than incumbents)
4. **Affordable** (vs enterprise solutions)
5. **Open platform** (API access)

### Messaging
- "Professional analytics for every level"
- "Track like the pros"
- "Your team's unfair advantage"
